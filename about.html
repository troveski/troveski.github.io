About

I'm Álvaro Patricio. I co-founded Zero Industries and work on Computer Vision and Deep Learning — building perception systems that let machines see, localize, and make sense of complex environments. I'm based in Toulouse, France.

This is where I write. Not about my work, exactly — more a set of meditations on living in modern times. Attention, machines, solitude, ambition, the small frictions of the everyday. I write to think clearly, and I publish in case it's useful to someone else.

You can reach me at alvaro@zeroindustries.eu or find me on GitHub.
